Plant Diversity at HKUST

Our campus boasts a rich variety of plant life, ranging from towering trees to low-lying shrubs and various herbaceous plants, forming a diverse ecosystem. These plants not only beautify the campus environment but also provide valuable resources for teaching and research for faculty and students. By observing and studying these plants, we can gain a deeper understanding of the mysteries of nature and explore how to coexist harmoniously with it.

Human and Nature

Interactions between human and nature are everywhere on our campus. Whether it is jogging in the early morning or taking a walk after class, faculty and students can feel the beauty of nature. The plants on campus not only provide us with fresh air, but also bring us peace of mind. By protecting and studying these plants, we can not only improve the ecological environment of the campus, but also contribute to sustainable development in the future.
