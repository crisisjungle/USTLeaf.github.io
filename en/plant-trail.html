One-World-Fountain

Outside the academic building is One-World-Fountain. Students say that if you walk into the fountain at sunrise and let the water wash over your body, you will achieve “unity with heaven and earth.” The garden combines modern landscaping with natural ecology concepts and is planted with a variety of aromatic herbs and ornamental flowers.
