About Us

USTLeaf 一葉知科

Our Mission

USTLeaf is dedicated to showcasing the rich plant resources on HKUST campus. Through digital means, it records, shares, and protects the campus's biodiversity. We hope that through this platform, more people will learn about the plant world on the HKUST campus and raise awareness of the natural environment and its protection.

We believe that understanding nature is the first step toward protecting it. By showcasing the diversity of plants on campus to faculty, students, and visitors, we hope to cultivate a love and sense of responsibility for the natural environment in more people, and work together to build a greener, more sustainable campus environment.

Our Team

USTLeaf Team is guided by Division of Environment and Sustainability at HKUST. It is composed of students from a variety of disciplines, including oceanography, environmental science, forestry, geography, computer science, and industrial design. Team members conduct field surveys, literature research, and utilize digital technology to jointly carry out the investigation, documentation, and presentation of campus plant resources.

Our team loves nature and is inclusive and open-minded. We welcome students from both inside and outside the university who are interested in botany and environmental protection to join us in enriching and improving this platform, and contributing to environmental protection in Hong Kong.
